First-Gen Job

First-Gen Job is a job search tool that aims to help first-generation immigrants:
1. Land their feet on the first job in the new country.
2. Explore the new job opportunity that fit their skills and background in their own cultural community
3. Get to know the company and salary information to avoid unfair treatment in the workplace.
4. Learn new skills that will help them in their future job position.

What's the problem?

The United States has a larger immigrant population, about 14.4% of the United States population are immigrants. Most immigrants came to the U.S. looking for a better life. However, there are many difficulties in their way of finding a job, such as language embarrassment or trade unfairly in the job place.

The Challenge

Design a tool that will help first-generation immigrants to find a job and help them to know if they are traded fairly in the workplace.

Outcome

  • 85% of users found it useful

Persona

Interviewing 14 people at the age from 16-56 who are first-generation immigrants, I identified 3 personas with different consumption habits and barries and examining their pain points.
❮ ❯

Key Finding

User Needs:

  • The employer has the same background as them or understands their situation as new immigrants.
  • Get to know the company and salary better to avoid unfair treatment in the workplace.
  • Learning necessary skills that help them to get into a job.

User Goals:

  • Find a job fits their background.

Opportunity

How might we create a job search tool that meets the needs of the first generation immigrants?

Final Thoughts

The participants of the initial user interview are all of the young generations which did not include the older generation. These two age ranges have very different user pain points which cause the first version did not solve the user pain points. This leads me to change a lot of detail in the project. Therefore, I learned that good user research is very important at the beginning.
