What's the problem?

The client is a new graduate student who does not have a lot of design projects and job experience.

Therefore, the client is looking for a showcase portfolio website that can best show the potential employer who she is and what she does. Also, the client wants the portfolio website to be user-friendly by providing clear navigation and a simple showcase for 3D animation works.

Opportunity

How might we create a portfolio website that can grab the attention of viewers without any big title projects being done?

Three Main Page

  • Connect with a top corner navigation system.
  • Home - Who you are.
  • Work - what you have done.
  • About - What makes you become you, and reach out!

Home Page

  • Landing page.

Work Page

  • Display Work - Display other works you have done.
  • Work Detail Page - Introduce more information about the work.

About Page

  • Never be afraid to show them who you are.
  • More Personalized Information - Let your visitors know more about you!
  • Contact Information - Don't ever forget to leave a contact.
