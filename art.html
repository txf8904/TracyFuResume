Chapter Seven

Overall Art Concept:

Chapter Seven is an adventure visual novel video game. The overall art style for game is a cutout art style on the characters, and a low poly shade vector with few color palettes for the game scenes. Since Chapter Seven is still a project under development, only part of the game art scenes is done.


Game Scenes:

Since the story is a little bit on the dark side, the tone of the art is a bit lighter and more colorful.
